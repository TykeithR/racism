Philando Castile

Philando Castile was in the car with his girlfriend and his four year old daughter when he was pulled over. His girlfriend was livesteaming on Facebook the whole situation. The incident happened when Castile told the officer he had a gun on him and the officer told him don't pull out your gun two times before he was reaching for it. The police officer claimed to feel 'threatened' and shot him in the arm 7 times. Philando was later rushed to the hospital and died 20 minutes later.

Trayvon Martin

Trayvon Martin was a 17 year old teen, who was walking into 7/11 to get a bag of Skittles and a can of Arizona iced tea. As he walked out the store, he encountered a man following him as he was walking home. His name was George Zimmerman amd he was the neighborhood watch volunteer. Zimmerman began to follow Trayvon because he was "suspicious to him, he had a hoodie on, and he looked like he was on drugs."

Oscar Grant

Oscar Grant was a 22-year old man who was shot on New Years Day coming from a party with his girlfriend. He was supposedly cited in a fight at the Fruitvale Train station in Oakland, California. The police officers detained him and his friends holding Oscar down to the ground face first. After being forced to lie down, Grant was shot in the back with a deadly pistol. He was unarmed. Grant was pronounced dead the next morning.
