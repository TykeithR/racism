Ruby Bridges was the first black child to go to an all white school. Bridges was born on September 8th, 1954, in Tylertown, Mississippi, and grew up on the farm with her parents and grandparents. When Ruby was 4 years old her parents Abon and Lucille Bridges moved to New Orleans hoping for a better life.


Ruby faced many challenges/obstacles:
  • She faced bullying and discrimination of her skin
  • Ruby was forced to take a test that majority didn't do well on
  • She couldn't play with the 'other' kids

    • On the otherhand, Ruby had many successes:
  • She was the smartest kid in her class
